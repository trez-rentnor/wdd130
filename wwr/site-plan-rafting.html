Overview

Purpose

White water rafting is one of the most exciting activities on earth. This page invites the public to share in that white-knuckle experience. We educate about the rivers available to raft, equipment available, and extend an invitation to mix and match all the elements of their trip, from travel to lodging to equipping and guiding their adventure.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

The target audience for this project is active adults who enjoy the outdoors. The target demographic is ages 25-35 with an income above $80,000. They enjoy activities like hiking and camping, but may not be experienced rafters.

What do they want?

Potential customers in this audience are internet savvy and, like everybody, always busy. They expect relevant information to be easy to find and clearly presented and that all services can be bundles in one inclusive package with a flexible, streamlined purchase process.

What are their needs that are note being met?

Opportunities exist in the unmet needs of streamlined planning for trips. Existing package deals are inflexible and are presented on a "take it or leave it" basis, with little room for customization.
